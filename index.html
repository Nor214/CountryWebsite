The Philippines is a unique country, and it is located in Southeast Asia. It’s known for its natural beauty, being the landscapes of amazement, For example we have the Chocolate Hills, located in Bohol. The Philippines is also acquainted with a rich culture influenced by its own history. It’s also considered a “megadiverse” nation, meaning that this country contains certain animals/species that are not around the world and are specific to the Philippines.
